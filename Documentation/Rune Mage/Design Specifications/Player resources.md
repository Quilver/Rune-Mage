1. Hitpoints: standard
2. Runes: There will be 16 runes, you unlock and increase your number of them during your playthrough. there will be 4 basic runes, 8 advanced runes, and 4 special runes
3. Cultivation/Wizard level: At the bonfire you can set your cultivation technique. This sets your wizard level (max number of runes to be combined into a spell), max number of runes for each rune and in total, and may offer additional benefits
4. Spells/Rituals: unique effect when activated with specific sequence of runes. Usually not suited for battle but provides utility (summon minions, stealth, break illusion, etc)
5. Ink: currency, can be used to inscribe runes. Bosses may drop unique inks for advanced runes.
6. Equipment(Wand, robes, hat?, rings): standard
7. Grimoire: Contains lore, spell, cultivation, or ritual. Lore will provide hints for some puzzle in the game
