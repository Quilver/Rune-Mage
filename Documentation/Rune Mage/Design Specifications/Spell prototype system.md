This is for a turn based game that is played on a grid. 


The player is a wizard that can cast the following spells:
1. projectile: starts at the player and ahead of the player until it hits something, effect is applied to hit thing
2. Earthquake: centered on the player, affects all NPCs in a radius
3. Buff: Applies effect to player
4. Cone: starts at the player and affects all NPCs in a cone in front of them
5. Wall: create wall in front of player that reflects projectiles
6. Hitscan: applies effect to nearest NPC in front of player

The effects of the spells can be changed and are the following:
1. Damage: reduce hp
2. Heal: increase hp
3. Push: move NPC
4. Status effect: Add or Remove tags from NPC
