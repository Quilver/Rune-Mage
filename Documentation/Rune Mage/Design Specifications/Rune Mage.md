Play as wizard journeying through the forgotten lands to find the rune of Apotheosis. The game is mashup of Magicka with Metroidvania and Soulslike elements. You will go to:

1. A Fortress hidden by illusions, filled with terracotta golems
2. A palace among the clouds
3. A house on chicken legs with a witch
4. A place of horrors beyond time and mind
5. Catacombs filled with the dead
6. A tower library filled with traps of the great arch mage
7. A forest filled with beasts and monsters

How the game plays:

1. Magicka: The game is top down with you fighting enemies or solving puzzles. You play as a wizard who interacts with the game world by combining together runes to create spells. This is inspired by the game Magicka with two changes, different and more rune types and limited casts for each rune
2. Metroidvania: The game will start the player off heavily limited (can only casts spells of one rune, can only use the fire and block runes, etc) and they will not have the tools to access most areas in the game (barriers requiring spells of at least 3 runes to break, illusions which need specialised spells to break, etc). Exploring will unlock powers ups that lets the player explore more areas
3. Soulslike: Runes have limited uses before being reset at the Bonfire(eg you can only use the heal rune 3 times). Bonfires also reset enemies and traps. Similar to dark souls 1 the goal is more about endurance than high intensity encounters. Similarly the map is divided into areas (interconnected) with bosses giving rewards

Tutorial (teach controls) → starting zone (streamlined version of main game, quickly gains the necessary tool to play) → main game loop → Final tower
